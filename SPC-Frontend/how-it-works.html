How It Works

Follow our simple process to join and operate within our pharmaceutical network

1

Initial Registration

Begin by completing our comprehensive online registration form. You'll need to provide:

  • Business registration documents
  • Pharmaceutical licenses
  • Tax registration details
  • Bank account information
2

Document Verification

Our team conducts thorough verification of all submitted documents:

  • License authenticity check
  • Business standing verification
  • Compliance assessment
  • Background checks
3

System Integration

Once verified, we'll help you integrate with our systems:

  • Account setup and configuration
  • Staff training sessions
  • System testing and validation
  • Initial inventory setup
4

Go Live

Start your operations with our network:

  • Begin receiving orders
  • Access real-time analytics
  • Utilize support services
  • Engage with the network
